Chameleon

Did you know that chameleons are born with special cells that have a colour or pigment in them?

  • Classification: Old World lizzard
  • Habitat: From rain forest to desert
  • Size: Up to 60 cm
  • Lifespan: Up to 10 years

Chameleons have very good eyesight for reptiles, letting them see small insects from a 5–10 meter distance. In fact, chameleons have the highest magnification (per size) of any vertebrate.
